More about Bonsai

History of Bonsai

The art of bonsai originated in China over a thousand years ago, and was later adopted and refined by the Japanese. Bonsai became popular in Japan during the Kamakura period (1185-1333), and has since spread to other parts of the world.

Types of Bonsai

There are many types of trees and plants that can be grown as bonsai. Some popular types include: juniper, pine, maple, elm, and ficus. Bonsai can also be classified based on their style, such as formal upright, informal upright, slanting, cascade, and semi-cascade.
